About Me

My name is Rihal Ignatova, and I am currently studying User Experience Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences. My journey started with sketching and drawing and now continues with wire-framing prototyping. Seeking new challenges that push me to my boundaries helps me learn about different aspects of design every day. I am always in pursuit to develop myself and intrigued with such ways I can improve my skill set. My dedication to arts and design has allowed me to experience cultures and how they interact with them. I am a huge believer in expressing thyself through various media. Having the quality skills set of doing it so, I assist clients in expressing their feelings as well as increasing their experience by making a product usable, enjoyable and accessible.
